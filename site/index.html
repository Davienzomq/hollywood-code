FAQ

What is Hollycode?▾
An open-source AI coding agent — a fork of opencode — that adds a stuntdouble cost router and Telegram remote control. It runs in your terminal, desktop, or IDE and connects to any LLM provider.
How does the model router work?▾
Every message is scored automatically. Simple chat is cast to a cheap stunt-double model; complex or production work is cast to the strongest star model your provider offers. Pin a model manually, or type /model auto to re-enable routing.
Do I need a paid AI subscription?▾
No. Hollycode comes with free models included. You can also connect your own subscriptions to Claude, GPT, Gemini, and more.
How do I control it from my phone?▾
Run /remote-control inside Hollycode. A short wizard pairs your Telegram account. After that you send tasks from your phone, approve permissions with buttons, and the bot keeps running after you close the terminal.
Is it open source?▾
Yes — Hollycode is open source under the MIT license, same as opencode. Contributions welcome on GitHub.
How is this different from opencode?▾
Hollycode adds the stuntdouble router (automatic per-message model selection), orchestration of parallel subagents, a /cost savings report, and full Telegram remote control — all native to the harness.
